# STAX Product Vision

STAX is a mobile-first platform designed to digitize, value, and exchange collectible trading cards.

The platform allows collectors to scan their cards using their phone, automatically identify the card, estimate its market value, and store it inside a digital vault.

STAX aims to reduce the friction between discovering a card, understanding its value, and completing a secure trade.

Core principles of the platform include:

• Mobile-first collector experience  
• Instant card recognition using AI  
• Transparent price discovery  
• Secure peer-to-peer trading  
• Collector network and discovery  
• Long-term digital record of collectible ownership

Over time the platform will evolve into a full collectible exchange, with escrow protection, breaker integrations, insurance options, and potentially tokenized collectible assets.

The long-term vision is to create the **central marketplace and data layer for the trading card ecosystem.**
