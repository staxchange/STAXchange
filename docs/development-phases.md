STAX development phases outlining the staged rollout of the platform.

Phase 1 – Core MVP
User accounts, card scanning, vault collections, price estimates, and basic trading.

Phase 2 – Marketplace Expansion
Listings, advanced search, collector following, activity feeds, and break room integration.

Phase 3 – Advanced Platform
Escrow protection, STAX index, insurance products, tokenization of collectibles, and analytics dashboards.
